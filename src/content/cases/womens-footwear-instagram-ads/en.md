## Project overview

A women's footwear brand selling exclusively through Instagram Direct — no e-commerce site, no fulfilment platform, just photos and DMs. The brief was to scale Direct-driven sales without breaking what already worked: the personal seller-customer dialogue.

## What we did

- Rebuilt ad-set structure around message-volume objectives (not site clicks)
- Created seasonal drop calendar tied to women's holidays (8th March / Valentine's / Mother's Day)
- Story-format creative pipeline — 9:16 vertical lookbook shots
- Layered traffic + reach campaigns to feed organic follower growth
- Set up automated DM responses for off-hours inquiries

## The result

12 campaigns running simultaneously delivering **102,174 total results, 2,129 link clicks at $0.16 CPC** on the strongest campaign. The brand's Direct conversion rate stayed above 18% throughout the engagement.
