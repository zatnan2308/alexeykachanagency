## Project overview

A premium anime apparel brand priced significantly above commodity hoodies on Aliexpress. The challenge: sell quality at full price to a fan-driven audience that knows where the cheap alternatives live. Creative had to do the heavy lifting.

## What we did

- Built character-by-character ad sets (Aki, Makima, Tishki, Чёрный Brother) — each fan group is its own audience
- Layered fan-page interest targeting with lookalikes from purchase data
- Created Instagram Shopping integration so users could buy without leaving the app
- A/B tested static product photos vs UGC video unboxings — UGC won every time
- Maintained a creative refresh cycle aligned with anime release calendar

## The result

17 active ad sets reaching 75,317 unique fans at 3.40% CTR. The brand established creative cadence aligned with new anime drops, turning fan releases into sales spikes.
