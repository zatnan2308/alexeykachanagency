## Project overview

A specialised training centre needed a measurable lead-generation system — not just lead volume, but lead quality tracked through to revenue. The brief was a Meta Ads programme integrated with the in-house CRM so cost-per-actual-customer (not just cost-per-lead) could be optimised.

## What we did

- Set up Meta Lead Ads with native instant-form integration
- Connected leads via Zapier webhook to the client's CRM in real time
- Tagged each lead with originating ad set, creative and audience
- Reported cost-per-lead AND cost-per-CRM-qualified-lead weekly
- Iteratively killed ad sets where qualification rate was below 30%

## The result

The optimised structure delivered FB leads at **$2.40-$2.59 cost-per-lead** across 9 active ad sets. CRM-qualified rate stabilised at 60%+, making the unit economics work at scale.
