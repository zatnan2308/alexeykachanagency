## Project overview

Brother Construction is a Calgary-based custom-build contractor specialising in garages, siding, roofing, concrete, decks and fences. The brief was a corporate site that explains the breadth of services without burying any single one — homeowners often start with "I need a deck" and end up with a whole exterior project.

## What we built

- Hero with embedded "Get a Free Quote" form
- Service grid: garage building, siding, roofing, concrete, basement development, decks & fences
- Live counters: 573 projects, 191 garage builds, 215 siding contracts, 164 roofing contracts
- "We are more than a construction company" mission section
- Mid-page "Get a Free Quote" CTA banner
- Featured-project carousel with photo + outcome description
- Footer with consultation request form for low-friction follow-up
