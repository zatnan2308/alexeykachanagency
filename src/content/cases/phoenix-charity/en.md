## Project overview

Phoenix Global Charity Foundation is a Polish non-profit running humanitarian missions for Ukraine, paediatric medical aid and environmental initiatives. The site needed to feel emotionally honest, surface impact in numbers and route donors to three different giving paths.

## What we built

- Three primary CTAs above the fold — "Donate", "Direct your 1% tax", "Active fundraisers"
- Live impact counters (donations made, missions completed, funds raised, people helped)
- Mission pages with photo storytelling
- Events calendar for charity events with registration
- Volunteer signup section
- News/updates feed with social sharing
