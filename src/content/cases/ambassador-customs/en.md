## Project overview

Ambassador Customs Brokerage is a licensed Canadian customs broker in Ottawa offering nationwide customs clearance — helping businesses and individuals import goods into Canada with fast, compliant CBSA submissions and transparent flat fees.

## What we built

- Authority hero — "Licensed Canadian Customs Broker — Nationwide Customs Clearance"
- Services: import clearance, CBSA submissions, CARM registration, RPP bond, HS-code classification
- Transparent flat-rate pricing section
- PARS status tracker plus free-quote and account-setup flows
- CARM guidance hub and blog
- 16-item FAQ covering clearance timelines, documentation and regulations

Built on WordPress with ACF Pro for the structured services, pricing and FAQ content.
