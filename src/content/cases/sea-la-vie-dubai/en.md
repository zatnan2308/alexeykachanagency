## Project overview

Sea la Vie Home Rental L.L.C. manages a curated selection of short-term vacation rental apartments across Dubai (Jumeirah Beach Residence, Al Dana Villas, Beach Walk). The site is built around fast browsing of listings and zero-friction WhatsApp inquiries.

## What we built

- Hero search bar with destination, check-in/out, guest count
- Featured listings grid with price-per-night, location, type
- Listing detail pages with photo galleries
- Multi-language toggle (EN/RU/AR) and currency switcher
- Travel guide blog feed (Holiday Ideas, Hospitality, Uncategorized)
- Concierge floating button — "Need help? Chat via WhatsApp"
- Footer-pinned WhatsApp CTA
