## Project overview

Global.life is a community-led editorial portal for Russian-speaking migrants navigating life in Switzerland — opening bank accounts, finding work, registering kids in school, buying property. The site needed clear category architecture and a "consult an expert" path for premium services.

## What we built

- Slider hero rotating between most-read articles
- Five top-level content sections (Articles, Migration, Tourism, Culture, Real Estate)
- Article cards with category colour-coding
- "Consult an expert" lead form on every category page
- Author profiles for credibility (each expert is a real Switzerland resident)
- Newsletter capture in footer
