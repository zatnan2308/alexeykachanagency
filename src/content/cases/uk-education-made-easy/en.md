## Project overview

UK Education Made Easy helps international students (with a special focus on Ukrainian refugees) apply to UK universities. The site walks prospective students through the entire path: courses → application → financing → success, with WhatsApp as the always-on contact channel.

## What we built

- Hero with Tower Bridge imagery and clear positioning ("Success is a decision. Make it.")
- Three programme tiles: HND (2 years), Bachelor (3-4 years), Master (1 year)
- "Become a student" inline lead form
- Five-stage application process explainer (study choice → documents → exam → finance → go to university)
- Document checklist (passport, residential status, NIN, school diploma, CV, personal statement)
- Financing block explaining UK Student Finance eligibility
- "Help for Ukrainians" dedicated section
- Universities & colleges partnership strip
