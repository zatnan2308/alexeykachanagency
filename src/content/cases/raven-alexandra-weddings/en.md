## Project overview

Raven Alexandra is a boutique wedding planning and design house serving couples in Calgary, Saskatoon and the Rocky Mountains. The aesthetic is "editorial magazine, not bridal blog" — every page reads like a Vogue spread. The site needed to attract the right kind of inquiry only.

## What we built

- Cinematic photography-led hero
- "Welcome" intro with cursive overlay typography
- "Our team" mid-page section humanising the planner
- "Our Approach" panel with framed photo
- Press-mention strip (Bronte Bride, Confetti, Rocky Mountain Bride, Style Me Pretty)
- Featured-shoots gallery with editorial captions
- "Let's connect" mid-page inquiry block (filtered language for high-end couples)
- Footer photo grid linked to social
