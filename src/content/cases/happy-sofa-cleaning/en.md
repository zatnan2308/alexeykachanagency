## Project overview

Happy Sofa YEG is a family-owned upholstery cleaning service in Edmonton, Alberta with a confident brand promise — "Life is Messy. We Make it Pure." Deep steam cleaning for sofas, sectionals, mattresses and carpets, with eco-friendly products that are safe for kids and pets.

## What we built

- Bold hero — "Life is Messy. We Make it Pure." — with dual CTA (free quote + WhatsApp)
- Trust strip: 500+ homes, 5★ rating, 100% eco-friendly, same-day availability
- Service cards: sofa & sectional, mattress sanitizing, pet-mess rescue, carpet refresh
- 4-step "How It Works" booking flow
- Before/after gallery with short video demos
- FAQ + free-quote form, with WhatsApp integration for instant contact

Built on WordPress with ACF Pro, so every block on the page is editable by the owner.
