Alexandre Protsenko is a Concordia-trained Quebec residential real-estate agent with 10+ years in finance (TD, CIBC, Laurentian, Scotia Bank) before moving to property. The site supports his personal brand with a valuation lead form and a long bio that earns trust.

- Long-form bio building on his banking & underwriting background
- Bilingual FR/EN throughout
- Click-to-call header with French Canadian phone formatting
- Connected to brokerage partner (Port and Mount Realties) for compliance
