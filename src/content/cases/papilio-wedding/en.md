## Project overview

Papilio is a bridal-couture house known for cinematic photoshoots and intricate dressmaking. The site needed to feel like flipping through a glossy magazine, not a shopping cart — every collection deserves space, every dress its own moment.

## What we built

- Full-bleed editorial hero per collection ("White Wind", "Summer Garden", "Colorful Dreams")
- Generous whitespace and serif typography throughout
- Collection-grouping navigation rather than a flat product list
- High-DPI photography optimised with AVIF/WebP
- Showroom appointment booking — no online checkout, the conversion is an in-person fitting
- Multilingual-ready architecture for showroom expansion
