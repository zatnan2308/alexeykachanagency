## Project overview

Yoline Industries delivers turnkey kitchen, bathroom and remodeling work to homeowners. The brief was a corporate site that builds trust through completed-project photography and routes quote requests directly to the project manager.

## What we built

- Hero with three core service tiles (kitchen, bathroom, drywall + painting)
- "Bringing your vision to life" approach section with personalised vs experienced framing
- Featured project showcase with inline before/after imagery
- Easy 4-step working process explainer
- Differentiator block (24/7 support, construction management, full-cycle, pre-construction)
- Embedded contact form + Google Maps for service-area transparency
