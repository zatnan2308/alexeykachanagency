## Project overview

American Home Blueprint with Alla is an online course teaching Russian-speaking immigrants how to buy their first home in the United States — "Buy your first home in America without fear or mistakes." Built for a licensed North Carolina realtor with 14+ years of experience.

## What we built

- Conversion-focused landing (in Russian) with pain-point-led messaging
- 7-module curriculum breakdown plus bonus materials
- Instructor bio and credentials (licensed Realtor, Keller Williams, NAR)
- Interactive mortgage-payment calculator
- Testimonials, FAQ and a 30-day money-back guarantee
- Secure registration with Stripe checkout

Built on Next.js for speed and image optimization, with Stripe handling payments.
