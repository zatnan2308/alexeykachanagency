## Project overview

Bcool ("We Are Best Cleaning Services & Solution") is a residential and commercial cleaning company operating since 2018. The site reinforces credibility through team profiles and clear subscription pricing — the goal is to convert visitors to recurring weekly/biweekly clients.

## What we built

- Hero with hovering helpline phone and CTAs
- Three-step process explainer (Find us fast → Choose services → Book appointment)
- "We have been doing projects since 2018" trust block
- Service grid: company, hospital, office, car cleaning
- Three-tier subscription pricing ($100 Basic / $150 Standard / $300 Premium)
- "Expert team" cards with photos and roles
- Embedded customer-feedback carousel
