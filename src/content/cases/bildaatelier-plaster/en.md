## Project overview

Bildaatelier crafts timeless interiors with Venetian plaster, decorative plaster and bas-relief wall art for high-end residential and commercial projects. The site needed to do one thing exceptionally: convince architects and homeowners that the work is worth the premium.

## What we built

- Bold hero with material-texture imagery and clear value proposition
- Three service pillars: Venetian plaster, decorative plaster, bas-relief
- Filterable "Gallery Works" section by technique
- "Why choose Bildaatelier" trust block (professionalism, individual approach, high-quality materials)
- Customer feedback section with named projects
- Single-CTA inquiry flow optimised for design-firm and homeowner leads
