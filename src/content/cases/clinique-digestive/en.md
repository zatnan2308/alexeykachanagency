## Project overview

Clinique Digestive is a Montreal wellness clinic combining colon hydrotherapy, massage therapy and aesthetic treatments. The site supports both promotional offers (20% off first session) and educational content explaining each treatment's medical context.

## What we built

- Promotional banner system for limited-time offers
- "Massothérapie" service tiles (Suédois, Californien, Profond, Ventouse)
- Educational explainer pages for each digestive-health treatment
- Mid-page session-booking CTAs (no friction between learning and booking)
- Pricing transparency on every service
- French-Canadian copywriting style throughout
