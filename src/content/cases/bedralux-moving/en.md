## Project overview

Bedralux Moving handles residential and commercial moves across the Greater Toronto Area — North York, Etobicoke, Mississauga, Niagara Falls, Hamilton, Ajax. The site had to communicate reliability quickly and pre-qualify moves before the phone call.

## What we built

- Hero with form-first lead capture (date, origin, destination)
- Service tiles: transportation, moving delivery, packing, long-distance, warehouse & storage
- "Why choose Bedralux" trust block (professional expertise, customised solutions, nationwide coverage, customer satisfaction)
- "Schedule your appointment today" mid-page CTA
- Testimonials with named clients
- Service-area footer with all GTA neighbourhoods
