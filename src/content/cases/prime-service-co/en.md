## Project overview

Prime Service Co. is an independent appliance-repair company serving homes and commercial kitchens across the Sacramento region — "Restoring order to your kitchen & laundry." Fast, reliable repair for both residential and commercial equipment.

## What we built

- Hero with Schedule Service + Call CTAs and a 4.9★ Google rating badge (189 reviews)
- Separate residential and commercial service cards with imagery
- HousecallPro online-booking integration
- "Areas we serve" coverage list + map
- Filterable "Our recent work" photo gallery by appliance type
- Brand-compatibility section (everyday, premium, commercial)
- FAQ accordion + a multi-step service-process explainer

Built on WordPress with ACF Pro; scheduling is handled through HousecallPro.
