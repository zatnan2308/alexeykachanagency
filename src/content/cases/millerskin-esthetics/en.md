## Project overview

MillerSkin is a solo medical-esthetics practice run by a licensed master esthetician with 13 years of experience. The brand voice is calm, expert and high-touch — the site needed to convert spa-curious visitors into booked first-time appointments.

## What we built

- Treatment cards with prices for each service (HydraFacial, Chemical Peel, Microdermabrasion, Microneedling, Dermaplaning, Men's Facials)
- "Book Your Appointment" CTAs surfaced in hero, mid-page and footer
- Skin-concern educational content for SEO
- Client review carousel
- Embedded online booking form with date/time/service selection
- Soft, premium colour palette (cream + gold) matching the in-clinic experience
