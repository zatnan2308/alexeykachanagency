## Project overview

A team selling specialised training courses for design / 3D-modelling software users (Photoshop, AutoCAD, 3ds Max etc) needed Meta lead-gen feeding their inside-sales reps. The challenge: keep CPL low enough that inside-sales unit economics work, while ensuring leads were intent-qualified, not curiosity-driven.

## What we did

- Targeted by software-interest groups and tool-of-trade lookalikes
- Built lead-form sequences that filtered out tire-kickers (skill-level question + intent question)
- Provided sales reps with weekly creative-attribution reports
- Killed creative variants below the qualification threshold within 7 days
- Maintained 11 simultaneous ad sets for portfolio resilience

## The result

Top-performing ad set delivered **565 leads at $1.42 cost-per-lead** on $803 spend. Sales-rep close rate improved 24% after creative-attribution feedback loop was introduced.
