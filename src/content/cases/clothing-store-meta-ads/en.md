## Project overview

A direct-to-consumer fashion brand needed help scaling Meta purchases without the cost-per-purchase climbing. The starting position: workable creatives, but no clear winner ad sets and no structured testing pipeline.

## What we did

- Restructured campaign architecture into purchase-optimised CBO with budget-tier audiences
- Built a creative-testing matrix (still / video / carousel × cold / warm / retargeting)
- Implemented Pixel + Conversions API for accurate attribution
- A/B tested over 30 creative variants across two months
- Scaled winners daily, killed losers within 48 hours

## The result

818 purchases at $3.50 cost per purchase, $2,859 total spend, **average ROAS 6.11×**. Top-performing ad sets delivered 8.88×, 10.64× and 5.98× — the campaign was profitable from week two and continues to scale.
