## Project overview

Green Line Property Management installs and services automated fire-alarm and fire-safety systems for commercial buildings. The site speaks to facility managers and procurement officers who need licence proof, transparent pricing and a single point of contact.

## What we built

- Hero with one CTA: "Order a calculation"
- "Why choose us" block: best prices, optimal solution, interior-friendly mounting, 12-month warranty
- "Why we are trusted" block: 24/7 work, equipment testing, on-time delivery, equipment in stock
- Equipment catalog with Bolid-brand integrations
- Service pages compliant with СП 5.13130.2009 standards
- Online quote request with object-type selector
