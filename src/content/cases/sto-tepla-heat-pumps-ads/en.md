## Project overview

STO Tepla installs high-ticket renewable-energy systems — heat pumps and solar panels for residential and small commercial buildings. At this price point, Meta Ads has to compete with direct B2B sales and trade-show activation. The brief: build paid-media into a primary lead engine that the sales team can rely on through weather-dependent demand cycles.

## What we did

- Comprehensive competitor analysis across regional installer market
- Multi-pixel implementation across the brand's installer-locator landing pages
- Connected Meta Ads to website analytics for full-funnel attribution
- Built creative pipeline showing real installations (homeowner permission obtained)
- Layered seasonal-demand bidding strategy (winter spike, spring lull, summer pre-purchase)

## The result

A consistently-running paid-media operation: **11.8M unique people reached, 220M impressions, $0.07 average CPC** on $318K total spend. Lead attribution stabilised so the client could plan installer hiring against expected lead flow.
