## Project overview

The Brest Center for Standardization & Metrology is a regional state institution offering metrological calibration, laboratory testing and product certification under Belarusian and ISO standards. The site serves both businesses (B2B services) and the general public (consumer information).

## What we built

- Slider hero communicating active programmes (e.g. "Natural Product" labelling)
- Three core service modules with deep-linked detail pages
- News feed with categorised filtering for industry updates
- "Готовность приборов" (instrument-readiness) online status lookup tool
- Service price calculator for tariff transparency
- Vacancies and project documentation sections for stakeholder transparency
