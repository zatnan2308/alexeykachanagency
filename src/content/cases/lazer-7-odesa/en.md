## Project overview

ЛАЗЕР · 7 (LASER · 7) is an Odesa laser-cutting and engraving studio — "We cut with light. We engrave with character." Nine years in, 5,000+ pieces shipped and a 48-hour average turnaround: souvenirs, décor, corporate merch and one-off custom work in plywood, acrylic, leather, glass, stone and metal.

## What we built

- Bold hero — "Ріжемо світлом. Гравіюємо з характером." — with quote-request and portfolio CTAs
- Trust strip: 9+ years, 5,000+ items made, 48-hour average turnaround
- Filterable portfolio gallery (20+ works) by category — souvenirs, décor, corporate, keychains, magnets, coasters, glassware
- Transparent pricing tables: cutting priced per metre of cut, engraving per dm² of surface
- A 13-material capability grid showing cut vs. engrave support — plywood, MDF, solid wood, acrylic, leather, glass, stone, metal and more
- Customer testimonial + a 9-question FAQ
- Location block for the "7 km" market with map, opening hours and parking notes
- Telegram / Viber / WhatsApp quick-contact + a "leave a brief" form that delivers straight to Telegram

Built on WordPress with ACF (custom fields) so the team edits portfolio items, materials and price rows themselves — no developer needed.
