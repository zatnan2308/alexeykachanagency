## Project overview

Vinyl4You is Ukraine's premium retailer of automotive vinyl wraps with both retail and bulk channels. The site needed a real catalogue (hundreds of SKUs), per-customer pricing for B2B partners, and a strong visual identity that communicates "premium" before any product is opened.

## What we built

- Cinematic hero with rotating product imagery
- Categorised catalogue with faceted filters (colour, finish, brand, dimensions)
- Search bar with auto-complete
- Product cards with price-match guarantee badge
- B2B "Wholesale purchases" section with separate login
- "Found it cheaper? We'll match" lead form
- Shipping calculator covering all Ukrainian regions
- Loyalty/bonus programme tracker for repeat buyers
