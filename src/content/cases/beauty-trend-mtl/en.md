## Project overview

Beauty Trend is Montreal's reference for bridal hair and makeup with a portfolio that has appeared in editorial photoshoots and high-end weddings. The site needed to feel as luxe as the service — soft palette, generous typography, photo-led layouts.

## What we built

- Soft-cream hero with editorial photography
- Service tiles (forfaits, bridal trial, group looks, photo session, vendor partnerships)
- Detailed forfait packages with inclusions
- Trusted-vendor partnership section
- "Pourquoi choisir Beauty Trend" trust block
- Bilingual French/English UI with locale-aware booking flow
- Google Reviews carousel embedded above footer
