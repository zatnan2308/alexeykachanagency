## Project overview

A yoga expert with strong organic content needed to monetise her audience through paid online courses. Previous campaigns were sporadic, run by media buyers who came and went. The brief: build a funnel the client could trust to turn ad spend into course sales without constant babysitting.

## What we did

- Audited previous campaign data and identified the highest-CTR creative formats
- Built a three-stage funnel: cold traffic → free webinar → paid course
- Created video-first creative pipeline showcasing the expert's signature poses
- Tested geo-targeting variants (EU traffic vs broad)
- Set up daily reporting dashboard so the client could see results without DM-ing me

## The result

A reliable, profitable Meta Ads funnel running across 12+ ad sets with 4.53% average CTR and $0.21 CPC. Course sales went from sporadic to predictable monthly revenue.
