## Project overview

A Philadelphia-based pre-owned vehicle broker focused on serving buyers with credit challenges. The site needed to qualify leads quickly and build trust around financing terms — down payments from $495, 172-point inspections, transparent pricing.

## What we built

- 4-step pre-qualification form (auto info → residential → employment → finish)
- Trust-first hero with phone, "Get approved" CTA and certified-pre-owned badging
- "Why choose us" sections targeting buyers nervous about used-car purchases
- Down-payment promo block above the fold
- Mobile-first responsive layout — most leads arrive on phones
