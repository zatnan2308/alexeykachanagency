## Project overview

Makkov LLC handles appliance repair across the Philadelphia metro — refrigerators, ovens, washers, dryers, dishwashers, microwaves. Customers usually need same-day help and arrive on the site stressed. The job of the page is to put a phone number in their hand within 2 seconds.

## What we built

- Top-bar with phone, email and opening hours always visible
- Hero CTAs: "Book a service" and "Call now" side-by-side
- Service grid with one card per appliance type (deep-link landing pages)
- Trust block: 20,000 happy customers, 2 states, 30+ in-house specialists
- Brand strip (Bosch, Frigidaire, Haier, etc.) for credibility
- Testimonials with named technicians
