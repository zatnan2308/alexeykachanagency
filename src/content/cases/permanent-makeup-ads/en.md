## Project overview

A permanent-make-up salon group running for several years with a steady but unscaled paid-ads programme. The brief was to professionalise the entire performance-marketing stack: from competitor analysis through to chatbot-driven booking automation.

## What we did

- Deep competitor analysis across the salon's regional market
- Audited and rebuilt all targeting layers around verified custom audiences
- Implemented Manychat-style automated booking via Instagram Direct
- Connected web analytics to ad-account data for full-funnel attribution
- Structured the account into 64 campaigns by procedure (brows / lips / areola / training)

## The result

A stable, fully-attributed paid-ads engine running at scale: **2.4M impressions, 206K unique reach, $0.06 CPC** on the strongest campaigns. The salon's booking pipeline now runs on autopilot through Direct.
