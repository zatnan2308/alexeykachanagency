## Project overview

Black Diamonds Vail provides luxury private transportation for resort travellers, corporate VIPs and special events. The site sells the experience first — fleet quality, "incredible comfort", trip planning — and only then asks for a booking.

## What we built

- Hero rotator: event transport / airport transport / local transport
- Premium fleet showcase (Cadillac Escalade ESV, GMC Yukon XL, Mercedes Sprinter, Mercedes Benz Sprinter Van)
- "Incredible comfort" full-bleed image section
- Trip-quality trust block (luggage by chauffeur, fuel for unhurried travel, high safety norms, professionalism)
- "Rates & Destinations" grid with one card per route (resort transfer, airport, charter)
- "To the airport with maximum comfort" service detail page
- Always-visible booking CTA with destination dropdown
