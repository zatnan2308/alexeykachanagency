## Project overview

The client managed a portfolio of 22 active Facebook campaigns with mixed results — some profitable, others bleeding budget. The brief: stop the bleeding, double down on winners and structure the account for repeatable scale.

## What we did

- Audited every active campaign — paused 6 unprofitable ones, consolidated 4 fragmented ones
- Implemented systematic creative refresh cadence (every 14 days)
- Layered lookalike audiences seeded from top 5% purchase data
- A/B tested landing-page variants for the top three traffic-receiving campaigns
- Deployed automated rules for budget reallocation based on ROAS thresholds

## The result

Across the portfolio, average purchase ROAS settled at 5×, with the top-performing campaign hitting **9× peak ROAS** after creative-and-audience refresh. The optimisation framework was documented and handed off so the in-house team could maintain it.
