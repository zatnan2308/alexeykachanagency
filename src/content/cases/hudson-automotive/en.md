## Project overview

Hudson Automotive ("Driven by Craftsmanship, Powered by Passion") is a northeast NJ auto body shop specialising in frame work, painting, polishing, dry cleaning, ceramics/liquid glass coatings and paint supplies. The site is dominated by a single message: see the work, judge for yourself.

## What we built

- Hero with "Elevate your ride to perfection" + 3-pillar trust strip (best painting, professional team, best quality)
- Six service tiles with deep-link detail pages
- Massive "Cases" before/after gallery — 20+ completed restoration thumbnails
- "Production of PPG paint" technology section (efficient, economical, high quality)
- "Evolution of an icon" colour-capability narrative
- Reviews block + map + contact form below the fold
