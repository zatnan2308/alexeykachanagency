## Project overview

HamersFix is a same-day appliance-repair company in Bethlehem, GA, serving 14 cities across Gwinnett, Barrow and Athens counties — "Appliance repair, done right — same day." 15 years in business with EPA-certified technicians.

## What we built

- Urgency-first hero with a live "next available slot" indicator and a ZIP coverage checker
- Service menu by appliance with plain-language problem descriptions
- 3-step "How It Works" process
- Trust badges: 4.9★ / 1,247 reviews, A+ BBB, Google Guaranteed, EPA 608, licensed & insured, 1-year warranty
- Premium brand roster (Sub-Zero, Wolf, Viking, Thermador, Miele…)
- Commercial B2B track + financing (Wisetack / Synchrony)
- Reviews + FAQ

Built on WordPress with ACF Pro for the modular service and trust blocks.
