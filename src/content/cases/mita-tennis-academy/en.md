## Project overview

MITA Tennis Academy ("Just tennis it") is a professional tennis-training program in Metro Denver, led by head coach Max Ivchenko with 30+ years of experience. Structured, results-driven coaching for kids, teens and adults across every USTA level.

## What we built

- Cinematic "Just tennis it" hero over a court video still
- Age- and level-based program tiers (Red / Orange / Green Ball, teens, adults, college prep)
- Coach roster with profiles and credentials
- 14 five-star testimonials + partner logos
- "Book a lesson" contact flow powered by WPForms
- Blog + Instagram / TikTok integration

Built on WordPress + Elementor, with custom HTML/CSS sections for the bespoke hero and program cards.
