## Project overview

DenCO is a family-owned Denver appliance repair business with a clear positioning: same/next-day service, certified technicians, 90-day warranty, transparent pricing. The site puts the inquiry form right in the hero so the conversion happens before any scrolling.

## What we built

- Hero with always-visible inquiry form (name, email, phone, service)
- Three-pillar trust block: same/next-day repair, local honest service, certified technicians
- "Book online OR Call now" toggle CTA
- Comprehensive appliance-by-appliance service grid (commercial refrigeration, microwave, refrigerator, gas/electric stove, dishwasher, washer, dryer)
- Customer testimonials with Google attribution
- Sticky phone-call CTA on mobile
