## Project overview

QBody by Aleksandra Khavanskaia is a personal-trainer brand built on 17+ years of experience and 1,000+ recovered clients. Aleksandra is a competing NPC athlete with multiple certifications (NASM, CES, PES, CSCS, CAFT). The site sells specialised recovery courses (post-mammoplasty, post-C-section) plus in-person training.

## What we built

- Dark, athletic hero with NPC-competition photography
- Two flagship course cards (Post-Mammoplasty Recovery $99, Post C-Section Recovery $99)
- Coach bio with full credentials
- "Personal Journey" green-card differentiator (5 surgeries self-rehabilitated, 30+ years training)
- "Real Transformations" client-results grid (named clients with measurable outcomes)
- Stripe-powered course checkout
- Multilingual (EN/RU) toggle
